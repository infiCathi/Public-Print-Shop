Print Made Easy

Who We Are

Since 2009 the Lennar Store has strived to help reduce the cost and production time for LENNAR Associates across the country. Providing a central location where Associates can go to find all of the materials and products that they need in one place. We've worked with multiple divisions within LENNAR to give them solutions to make their jobs easier. Printing materials, ordering products, allowing divisions to share marketing ideas through products on our store, customizing content on the store to help our fellow Associates succeed, and cutting costs so Associates can focus their money, time, and energy towards other aspects of their work.

And we wanted to bring that to the public, with Lennar Print Services.

What we do

Since 2010: We're here to make printing easy.

Lennar Print Services (LPS) is a one-stop shop for all printing needs. Our friendly and knowledgeable team are able to make the impossible possible! We offer quick turnaround times and high-quality products at a fraction of the cost, saving you an average of 63% when compared to name brand printers. We offer a wide variety of printing services including... well, everything! Instead of having you comb through thousands of pages of products, this brochure should give you a good idea of all we can create!

Here's a few things that we provide:

Printing

Business Cards, Flyers, Postcards, Notecards, Notebooks, Displays, Posters, POP Signs, Stickers, Vinyl Stickers, Window Clings, Door Hangers, Rack Cards, Bound Reports, Brochures, Menus, Tent Cards, Stationery, Letterhead, Envelopes, Books, Weather Proof Signs, Vinyl Banners, Fence Wraps, Invitations, anything that our Associates need..

Merchandizing

Drinkware, Shirts, Hats, Sweatshirts, Table Tents, Tote Bags, Paper Bags, Paper Cups, Napkins, Cutlery, Magnets, Mouse Pads, Stamps, Flags, Wayfinding Signs, Mugs, Towels, Golf Balls, Business Card Holders, Desk Calendars, Wall Calendars, Backpacks, Laptop Bags, Promo Items, Cutting Boards, Awards, Certificates, Framed Artwork, Engraved Pens, Balloons, Company Culture, and more.

Branding

Company Culture, Mascots, Lennar Blue shirts, jackets, pens, mugs, customized business cards, letterheads, and more. This is the place to get all of LENNAR's swag.

Designing/Editing

From product images, to headshots, business card re-design, postcards, we can help make sure the end result of your product comes out how you want it to.

Digitizing

Small format and wide format scanning, CD/DVD duplication and more--we can help you digitalize your files, making it easier to organize and search them.

Customizing

Packages, Bundles, and simply adding Products: we can customize the store to suit you and your team. Have something you order frequently? Let us put it on the store for you. Pre-set Templates for business cards, posters, and more can be placed onto the store for you to easily re-purchase when needed.

Special Services

Heat Transfer (apparel customization), Laser Engraving & Cutting (wood, metal, stone, acrylic), Dye Sub (coasters, wood, plastic), Vinyl (stickers), UV Printing (wood slices, name badges, white ink printing), Embroidery.

Finishing

From the start of the process, choosing paper type, material type, and making sure it comes out with the colors you wanted, all the way to the end: cutting, folding, packaging. We can stitch, staple, coil bind, laminate, mount, apply covers and backings, put in grommets and so much more.

Shipping/Packaging

Customizing how the item is packaged and shipping for you, we want to make sure that you receive it when and how you need it. We can process rush orders for you, and work with you on the type of service you need.

Not listed?

Anything that you need, any idea that you have, let us know and we can make it happen. We have yet to find a product we haven't been able to create. We source and work with vendors to make sure that we can accomplish what our Associates needs.
