My Account

    Troubleshooting

    Issues with the website?

    Lennar® Print Services works best on Google Chrome. While on Chrome, if the website is still not uploading please contact Customer Service to see if they are having technical difficulties. Feel free to call them at (949) 349-8220.

    If your file is not uploading, lets first try troubleshooting your computer using the steps below:

    1. Make sure you are ordering on Google Chrome as this is the ideal browser for using Lennar® Print Services.
    2. Make sure the pop-up permissions are set to allow Lennar® Print Services pop-ups.
    3. Clear you history/cache. Once you clear your history, close the browser, then reopen the browser and log back into your account to order.
    4. If none of the steps above work please contact Customer Service at (949) 349-8220 or e-mail them at lennarprintservices@lennar.com

    There are many conflicts that could be stopping a file from uploading. While choosing your details for your job keep in mind the file you are going to upload. For example, if you choose that your file is 2 pages but it is only 1 page you will get an error when uploading the file.

    Here are the most common reasons for errors and their solutions:

    • Size not matching up - This may be because your pdf is built with bleeds which could make the pdf a quater of an inch to a half inch larger than the size you actually need. If your file does have bleeds make sure your item details reflects this.
    • Page count doesnt match - Make note of how many pages are in your document and then update the item details.

    If you didn't receive your order confirmation e-mail please contact Customer Service at (949) 349-8220 and a new one can be sent to you.
